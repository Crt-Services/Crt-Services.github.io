Ultrasonic Flow Meters Gas & Liquid Training

Details:

This training provides comprehensive instruction for using Ultrasonic Flow Meters in both Gas & Liquid applications.

Course Syllabus:

Day 1: Theory of Operation
  • Ultrasonics – How it works and basic equations behind the technology
  • Flow measurement methods - transit time vs. doppler
  • Methods of timing measurement (zero crossing – cross correlations)
  • Real signals and discussions
  • Style of ultrasonic sensors
  • Electronics - sample rates and flow measurement rates
  • Velocity measurement and average velocity determinations – how to get the whole average with just a few measurements
  • Flow Measurement Uncertainty – A Deep Dive
  • Types of meters – Benefits and Deficiencies
    • Clamp-on
    • Gas
    • Liquids
    • One path, two path… three path, four… and so on.
    • The good, the bad and the ugly – types of meters
Day 2: Technology In Action
  • Hands on demos – (class participates in breaking meters)
  • Weak signals
  • Zero biases
  • Low SNRs
  • How hydraulics affects meters
  • Hands on demo with 8 path (4 chord) meters and 14 chord meters
  • Elbows/Tees and Flow Conditioners
  • Why use flow conditioners – what goes wrong without FCs and with FCs
  • How meters go sideways
  • Dirt effects upon measurement
  • Clogging an FC
  • Condition based monitoring – what it does and doesn’t do.
