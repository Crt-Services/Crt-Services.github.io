Training Two: Gas Pipeline Maintenance

This training program focuses on the essential skills and knowledge required for the maintenance of gas pipelines. Participants will learn about inspection techniques, repair procedures, and safety measures to ensure the integrity and reliability of pipeline systems.

Key Topics Covered:

  • Pipeline inspection methods
  • Corrosion prevention and control
  • Leak detection and repair
  • Pipeline safety regulations
  • Emergency response procedures

Location: Training Center B, Suburbia
